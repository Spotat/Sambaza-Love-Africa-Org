Sambaza Means “Share” — And We Share Love

Sambaza Love Africa is a non-profit organization based in Nairobi, Kenya, dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable children and women. We believe that every child deserves a safe home, every woman deserves dignity, and every person deserves a chance to thrive.

Street Children Support

Women Empowerment

Education Access

Sponsoring school fees, uniforms, and supplies for children in homes and low-income communities.

Community Outreach

Partnering with local leaders and volunteers to identify and uplift the most vulnerable.

Empowering Street Children in Nairobi Through Daily Meals, School Support, and Safe Spaces That Restore Dignity and Hope

Every child deserves to wake up knowing they’ll eat, learn, and be safe. At Sambaza Love Africa, we walk the streets of Nairobi to meet children where they are — offering hot meals, school supplies, hygiene kits, and mentorship. We don’t just feed bodies — we nourish futures, helping children transition from survival to education, and from isolation to belonging.

Breaking the Cycle of Poverty for Women Through Skills Training, Mentorship, and Psychosocial Support Rooted in Compassion

Women in vulnerable communities often carry the weight of entire families — with little support. We equip them with practical skills, emotional healing, and peer networks so they can rebuild their lives with confidence. Whether it’s tailoring, financial literacy, or counseling, our programs are designed to restore dignity — one woman, one step, one story at a time.

Building Stronger Communities by Partnering With Local Leaders, Volunteers, and Families to Create Sustainable Change From the Ground Up

Change doesn’t come from outside — it grows from within. We collaborate with community elders, teachers, and grassroots volunteers to identify needs, co-design solutions, and ensure long-term impact. From neighborhood feeding programs to parent-teacher forums, we believe lasting transformation happens when communities lead — and we walk beside them.

Ensuring Every Child in a Home or on the Streets Has Access to Education, Uniforms, Books, and a Believing Adult Who Shows Up

Education is the greatest equalizer — but for many children in Nairobi, it’s out of reach. We remove barriers: paying school fees, providing uniforms, delivering books, and connecting kids with mentors who believe in them. Because no child should miss school because they can’t afford a pair of shoes — or because no one told them they matter.

Your Gift Changes Lives — Every Shilling Reaches a Child, a Woman, a Future

The programs we run to help the people

Comprehensive Support and resources for vulnerable populations

Counseling and Psycho-Social Support

We provide ongoing mental health counseling and psycho-social support to beneficiaries and caregivers to promote emotional healing and resilience.

Emergency Shelter and Rescue Services

Our 24/7 volunteer network responds to critical cases involving children, women, and men in crisis, offering immediate shelter and guidance.

Education and Adult Literacy

We conduct regular seminars, training sessions, and adult education programs to improve literacy and life skills, empowering individuals to become self-reliant.

Volunteer Integration & Peer Mentorship

Graduates of our programs are encouraged to become volunteers, mentoring others and continuing the cycle of healing and empowerment.

Employment and Economic Empowerment

Through volunteer networks, we help create meaningful employment opportunities and financial literacy programs, including table banking groups for sustainable income generation.
